About

My name is OLADOTUN JOSEPH OJEBODE,known as Whirl Josh in the wave community. I am from Nigeria.I won WAVEBUILDER WAVEKING OF OCTOBER 2013.I designed this small website based on what have learnt so far in the game from the vets and elites and from studying my hair with the aim of helping upcoming wavers or people who have interest in waving and and doesnt know how to go about it.I believe this site here should get you started. In addition the various links to the facebook wave pages and youtube videos should help you in accomplishing your wave Goals.Just a little word of advice here.You want something,you go after it,not only in this wave game but in all life endeavour,Most of the important things in this world have been accomplished by people who have kept on trying when there seemed to be no hope at all,there are some things in life that may come easy but most things worth having or achieving will only come with dedication and tenacity to get it.Stay true to your game and believe in yourself,dont get discouraged by the other wavers hair texture,words or reaction.SHOUTOUT to all wavers,the LEGENDS,the VETERANS,the ELITES, the ROOKIES.Above all,i give thanks to the almighty GOD.
